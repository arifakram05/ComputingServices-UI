Computing Services manages the University computer labs, develops and maintains the configurations of the laptop and desktop PCs, handles the PC software site licenses, runs the Novell servers, and provides general support for PCs at the University. The main computer labs are located in Dickinson Hall and University Halls on the Metropolitan campus, and in the Dreyfuss building on the College at Florham.
